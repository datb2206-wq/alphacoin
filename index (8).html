HOW IT WORKS

Proof-of-Inference

Forget useless hashing. AlphaCoin rewards bots that generate real trading value — on-chain, every epoch.

Each epoch, trading bots receive a market analysis challenge — price data, signals, conditions. Real problems, not arbitrary hashes.

Bots analyze the data and submit trading signals or strategies. The quality of inference is scored against real market outcomes.
